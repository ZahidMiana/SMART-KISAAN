    Crop Disease Information

    Protect your crops with expert knowledge.

    Early Blight (Tomato)

    Symptoms: Dark spots with concentric rings on leaves, yellowing, and fruit rot.

    Prevention: Crop rotation, proper spacing, and fungicides.

    Rust (Wheat)

    Symptoms: Orange pustules on leaves, reduced photosynthesis.

    Prevention: Resistant varieties, timely fungicide application.

    Smut (Sugarcane)

    Symptoms: Black whip-like structures, stunted growth.

    Prevention: Healthy seed canes, hot water treatment.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does Smart Kisaan help farmers? +
    Smart Kisaan provides real-time weather updates, crop price trends, livestock management tips, and AI assistance to help farmers make informed decisions and improve productivity.
    Can I get weather updates for my city? +
    Yes! Enter your city name in the Weather Updates section to get real-time weather data, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more, to plan your farming activities.
    How can I contact the AI Assistant? +
    Simply type your farming-related question in the AI Assistant section, and our AI will provide practical advice on crops, pests, weather impacts, and more.
    How can I improve soil health for better yields? +
    To improve soil health, use organic compost, rotate crops, and avoid over-tilling. Adding cover crops like clover can also enhance soil nutrients and reduce erosion.
    What crops are best for small farms in Pakistan? +
    For small farms in Pakistan, consider high-value crops like vegetables (tomatoes, onions), herbs (coriander), or fruits (mangoes). They require less space and can be sold at local markets for good profit.
